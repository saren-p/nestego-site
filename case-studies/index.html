Industry case studies

These published case studies show how organizations modernized operations with cloud platforms. SMB teams can borrow the same playbook: reduce friction, simplify systems, and focus on measurable outcomes.

Legal / Professional Services

Minster Law: cloud migration to simplify operations

Situation: Minster Law needed to modernize aging infrastructure while keeping critical systems dependable for legal teams and day-to-day client service.

What changed: The organization migrated infrastructure and a key application to Microsoft Azure. The customer story reports up to a 300% boost in employee productivity on some tasks and a 20% reduction in costs.

Why it matters to SMBs: Moving priority workloads first can improve team speed and lower operating overhead without waiting for a full platform rebuild.

Healthcare

Franciscan Health: Epic EHR migration on Azure Virtual Machines

Situation: Franciscan Health needed a scalable infrastructure approach for core clinical systems while controlling long-term technology costs.

What changed: The health system migrated Epic EHR to Azure Virtual Machines optimized for Epic. According to the published customer story, the program is saving $45 million.

Why it matters to SMBs: Even in highly regulated environments, structured migration projects can improve resilience and produce meaningful cost savings when aligned to essential workloads.

Source
E-commerce / Platform Modernization

Yahoo: modernization with AWS to improve delivery speed

Situation: Yahoo’s e-commerce operations needed stronger platform efficiency and faster release cycles to support competitive digital product delivery.

What changed: The AWS case study cites 30–40% operational savings and 30% faster time to market following modernization work.

Why it matters to SMBs: Platform improvements are not only technical upgrades—they can directly affect cost structure and the pace at which new customer-facing improvements launch.
