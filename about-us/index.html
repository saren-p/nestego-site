Our Story

Founded in the heart of Montreal, Nestego was born from a simple observation: small and medium businesses deserve enterprise-grade IT support and modern web presence without the enterprise price tag.

Nestego is Montreal-led and scales through a trusted network of specialists. We've helped hundreds of businesses across Quebec and Canada stabilize their IT infrastructure, secure their data, and establish powerful digital identities.

Our philosophy is simple: your success is our success. We don't just fix computers or build websites; we become an extension of your team, ensuring technology drives your growth instead of holding it back.
